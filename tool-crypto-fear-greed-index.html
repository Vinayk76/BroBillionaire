‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Crypto Fear and Greed Index? ‚ñº
The Crypto Fear and Greed Index is a sentiment indicator that measures the emotions of crypto investors on a scale of 0-100. A value of 0 means "Extreme Fear" indicating potential buying opportunities, while 100 means "Extreme Greed" suggesting the market may be due for a correction. It helps investors understand market psychology and make informed decisions.
How accurate is the Fear and Greed Index? ‚ñº
The index is a useful sentiment indicator but should not be used as the sole basis for investment decisions. Historically, extreme fear readings have often preceded market rebounds, while extreme greed has preceded corrections. However, markets can remain irrational longer than expected, so always combine this with other analysis.
When is the best time to buy Bitcoin based on this index? ‚ñº
Generally, readings below 25 (Extreme Fear) have historically been good buying opportunities. This is when most investors are selling in panic, creating potential value for long-term investors. However, never invest more than you can afford to lose and consider dollar-cost averaging rather than lump-sum buying.
Does the Fear and Greed Index work for altcoins? ‚ñº
The index is primarily based on Bitcoin data, but since Bitcoin leads the overall crypto market, it's a good proxy for general crypto sentiment. Altcoins typically move in correlation with Bitcoin, especially during major market moves, making this index relevant for the broader crypto market.
