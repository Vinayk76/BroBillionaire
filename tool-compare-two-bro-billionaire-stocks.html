FAQ

Should I pick the winner and ignore the loser?

No. Past performance doesn't predict future returns. Nvidia crushed Tesla historically, but if EV adoption accelerates or chip demand slows, roles could reverse.

Why do high-return stocks have bigger drawdowns?

Volatility cuts both ways. High-growth stocks swing wildly. You can't get Nvidia's +2,418% returns without enduring its -66% crashes.

Can I own both stocks?

Yes! Diversification across Bro Billionaire stocks reduces single-stock risk. A portfolio of all 7 would have smoother returns than any individual stock.
