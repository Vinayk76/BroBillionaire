FAQ: Your Questions Answered

Which crypto should I buy today?

Start with Bitcoin (40-60% of your portfolio) and Ethereum (20-30%). These are the safest long-term bets. Add Solana (10-15%) and a small allocation (5-10%) to higher-risk plays like LINK, XRP, or AVAX only after you understand them.

Is it too late to invest in crypto in 2026?

No. Global crypto adoption is still under 6%. We're in the post-halving bull cycle which historically offers the best returns. Institutional adoption via ETFs is accelerating. The opportunity is still massive, but manage your expectations ‚Äî 10-100x gains are rarer now than in 2017.

How much money do I need to start?

You can start with as little as $10-$100. Most exchanges have no minimum. However, investing small amounts means fees eat into your profits. We recommend starting with at least $500-$1,000 if possible, and only money you can afford to lose entirely.

Which crypto will give 1000x returns?

Honestly? We don't know, and anyone who claims they do is lying. 1000x returns require: (1) extremely low market cap (<$10M), (2) perfect timing, (3) massive luck. For every 1000x coin, there are 1,000 that went to zero. Focus on 5-20x opportunities with solid fundamentals instead.

Should I invest in meme coins?

Only with money you're 100% okay with losing. Meme coins (DOGE, SHIB, PEPE, etc.) can 10-100x quickly but also crash 99% overnight. If you must play memes, limit it to 1-5% of your crypto portfolio. Never chase pumps. Get in early or don't get in at all.
