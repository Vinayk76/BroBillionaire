Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

Our Commitment: BroBillionaire respects your privacy. We collect minimal data, don't require registration, and never sell your personal information to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

We collect very limited information:

  • Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This includes pages visited, time spent, device type, and approximate location (country/city level).
  • Contact Form Data: If you contact us, we collect your name, email, and message content to respond to your inquiry.
  • Technical Data: Standard server logs including IP address, browser type, and referring pages for security and performance monitoring.

2. Information We Don't Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Financial information or trading account details
  • Social security or identity numbers
  • Credit card or payment information
  • Personal trading history or portfolio data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Improve our website content and user experience
  • Respond to your inquiries and messages
  • Analyze traffic patterns and popular content
  • Maintain website security and prevent abuse

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies for:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics cookies to understand site usage
  • Preferences: To remember your preferences (like dark mode)
  • Performance: To optimize page load times

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work properly.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics (Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Google Fonts: For typography
  • Cloudflare: For CDN and security services

6. Data Retention

Analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics' default settings (26 months). Contact form messages are kept for as long as necessary to respond to inquiries and then deleted.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics tracking
  • Disable cookies in your browser

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. Trading and investing should only be done by adults who understand the risks involved.

9. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. Our website uses HTTPS encryption for all connections.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
