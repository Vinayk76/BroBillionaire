Understanding IPO Metrics

GMP (Grey Market Premium): Unofficial premium over issue price. High GMP = strong listing expected. Not guaranteed.

QIB (Qualified Institutional Buyers): Large institutions. >3x subscription = good demand.

NII/HNI (Non-Institutional Investors): High net-worth individuals. >5x subscription = strong HNI interest.

Retail: Individual investors. >2x subscription = good retail demand.

Subscription Rate: Times oversubscribed. Higher = more demand than shares available.
