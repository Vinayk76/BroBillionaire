- [ ] Revenue is growing 20%+ annually

- [ ] Management team is competent and aligned with shareholders

## âœ… CRASH SURVIVAL CHECKLIST (Use During -30%+ Drawdowns)

### When Your Position is Down 30-50%
- [ ] Has the business fundamentally changed? (Y/N)
- [ ] Is revenue growth still strong? (Y/N)
- [ ] Is this a market-wide selloff or company-specific? (Market / Specific)
- [ ] Am I being forced to sell (margin call, need cash)? (Y/N)
- [ ] Do I have additional capital to add at these levels? (Y/N)

### Decision Framework
**If fundamentals are INTACT:**
- [ ] Hold current position
- [ ] Add more if I have capital
- [ ] Set price alert for -20% more (potential add point)

**If fundamentals are BROKEN:**
- [ ] Admit I was wrong
- [ ] Cut position by 50% immediately
- [ ] Reassess thesis completely
- [ ] Consider full exit if thesis is dead

**RED FLAG:** If you're selling purely because of price (not fundamentals), you may regret it.

## ðŸš¨ RED FLAGS - WHEN TO SELL

### Company-Specific Red Flags
- [ ] Revenue growth drops below 10% (for growth stocks)
- [ ] Gross margins compressing 500+ bps
- [ ] Losing market share to competitors rapidly
- [ ] CEO/founder departs unexpectedly
- [ ] Major accounting scandal or restatement
- [ ] Product recall or critical failure
- [ ] Regulatory action threatens core business

### Market/Valuation Red Flags
- [ ] Stock trading at >200x P/E with slowing growth
- [ ] Valuation 3x+ above historical average with no justification
- [ ] Insider selling accelerates (>50% of holdings)
- [ ] Short interest spikes >30% (bearish signal)
- [ ] Analyst downgrades from 10+ firms simultaneously

### Personal Red Flags
- [ ] You can't sleep because of position size
- [ ] You're checking price 10+ times per day
- [ ] Position grew to >40% of portfolio
- [ ] You need the money in next 12 months
- [ ] You've lost all conviction in thesis

**If you check 3+ boxes, strongly consider trimming or exiting.**
