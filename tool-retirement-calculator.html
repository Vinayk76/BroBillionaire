What is the 4% withdrawal rule?
The 4% rule states you can withdraw 4% of your retirement corpus annually (adjusted for inflation) for 30 years without running out of money. Based on the Trinity Study. Should I invest aggressively before retirement?
Yes, if you have 10+ years to retirement. Use 100-minus-age rule: If you're 30, keep 70% in equity. As you approach retirement, shift to 60% debt, 40% equity. This protects against sequence-of-returns risk while maintaining growth. Post-retirement, 70% debt + 30% equity is commonly recommended.
