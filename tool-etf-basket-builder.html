FAQ

How often should I rebalance my basket?

Annual: Most tax-efficient (long-term cap gains)
Quarterly: Better performance but more taxes
Never: Let winners run (highest returns but concentrated risk)

Can I automate this basket?

Some brokers (Fidelity, Schwab) offer basket trading. You can buy all 7 stocks in one order with specified weights. Or use fractional shares to manually build your basket.

What about dividend reinvestment?

Turn on DRIP (Dividend Reinvestment Plan) for Apple and Microsoft. This auto-buys more shares with dividends, compounding returns.
