Understanding Liquidation

  • Liquidation: Your position is automatically closed when losses approach your margin
  • Maintenance Margin: Minimum margin required (0.5% for most pairs)
  • Partial Liquidation: Some exchanges reduce position instead of full liquidation

Risk Management Tips

  • Never use more than 5-10% of capital on a single leveraged trade
  • Use stop-losses to limit downside before liquidation
  • Lower leverage = more room for price movement
  • High leverage (50x+) is extremely risky for volatile assets

Leverage Recommendations

  • 1-3x: Conservative, suitable for swing trading
  • 5-10x: Moderate risk, common for day trading
  • 20-50x: High risk, only for scalping with tight stops
  • 100x+: Extreme risk, not recommended
