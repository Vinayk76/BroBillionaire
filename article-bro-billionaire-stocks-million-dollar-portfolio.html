The Golden Rules: Risk Management That Saves Your Account

Rule #1: Never Use Leverage or Margin

This is a cash-only strategy. No margin. No options (unless you're experienced). One leveraged position during a crash = account wipeout. You're going for 100x over 10 years, not 10x in 1 year with 90% blowup risk.

Rule #2: Rebalance Annually, Not Monthly

Once per year, check your allocation. If Nvidia is now 50% of your portfolio, trim some and buy laggards. But don't over-trade. Let winners run, but don't let one stock become 70% of your net worth.
