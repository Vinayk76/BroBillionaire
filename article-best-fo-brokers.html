Battle-Tested Pro Tips

Before you create that account, these hard-won lessons from the trading trenches:

üí° Have a Backup Broker. Always.

Never keep all your capital with one broker. When one platform crashes (and it will, on the worst possible day), your backup saves you from disaster. Zerodha + Dhan is a popular combo.

üí° Test During Volatility Before You Commit

Open a demo account or trade with minimum capital during an expiry day. See how the platform handles stress. A broker that's smooth on calm days but crashes on expiry is worthless.

üí° Calculate Your REAL Cost

Brokerage is just one part. Add STT, GST, SEBI charges, stamp duty, and slippage. Use SEBI's brokerage calculator to see your true cost per trade. You might be surprised.

üí° Check Margin Requirements

Different brokers offer different margins. Some let you trade with less capital, others are stricter. Know this before you size up your positions.
