1. Overcoming Fear in Trading

Fear is the trader's greatest enemy. Fear of loss, fear of missing out, fear of being wrong - these emotions can paralyze you, make you exit winners too early, or prevent you from taking valid setups.

Key Insights

  • Fear comes from trading too large. Size down until you're comfortable.
  • Accept that every trade has a probability of loss. It's not personal.
  • Focus on process, not individual trade outcomes.
  • Fear diminishes with experience and proper risk management.
