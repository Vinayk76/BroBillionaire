Pre-Market Trading Strategy

Gap Up Strategy: Wait for first 15-min candle. If breaks high with volume = buy. If fails = reversal trade.

Gap Down Strategy: Selling pressure. Wait for bounce near support or gap fill attempt.

ORB (Opening Range Breakout): First 15-min high/low. Breakout = strong momentum move expected.

Volume Confirmation: Always check if gap is backed by news and high volume.

Risk Management: Gaps can fill quickly. Use tight stop losses. Don't chase extended gaps.
