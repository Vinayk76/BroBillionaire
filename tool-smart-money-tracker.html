Understanding Smart Money

FII (Foreign Institutional Investors): Foreign funds buying/selling Indian stocks. Positive FII = bullish market.

DII (Domestic Institutional Investors): Indian mutual funds, insurance companies. Often counter FII flows.

Bulk Deals: Large transactions (0.5%+ equity). Indicates big player interest in specific stocks.

Block Deals: Off-market institutional trades. Usually signals major position changes.

Promoter Buying: Insiders buying own stock = bullish sign. Selling = caution unless for known reasons.
