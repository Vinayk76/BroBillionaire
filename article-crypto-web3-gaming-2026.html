Getting Started Guide

1
Set Up Wallet
Install MetaMask or a game-specific wallet. Secure your seed phrase properly. Some games have their own wallets for easier onboarding.
2
Get Gas Tokens
You'll need the native token of the game's blockchain for transaction fees. ETH for Ethereum games, SOL for Solana, MATIC for Polygon, etc.
3
Research Games
Watch gameplay videos. Read tokenomics. Check Discord communities. Start with free-to-play games before investing.
4
Start Playing (Free)
Many games now offer free starter characters or rental systems. Learn the game before spending money.
5
Join Community
Discord and Twitter are where alpha happens. Learn strategies from experienced players. Consider joining a guild for support.
6
Scale Gradually
Only invest more after you understand the game economy. Track your earnings vs. time spent. Treat it as entertainment first.

Red Flags to Avoid

  • Unrealistic earning promises ‚Äî "Earn $1000/week!" is almost always a scam or unsustainable
  • Mandatory NFT purchase to play ‚Äî High entry barriers often mean early players dump on late ones
  • No actual gameplay ‚Äî If it's just "click and earn," it's a ponzi, not a game
  • Anonymous team ‚Äî Legitimate games have known developers with track records
  • Token-first, game-second ‚Äî Good games focus on fun; earning is supplementary
  • Unsustainable APY ‚Äî 1000% APY means massive inflation diluting your rewards
