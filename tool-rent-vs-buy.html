When to Rent

  • You might relocate for career in next 5 years
  • Job stability is uncertain
  • You want flexibility and no maintenance hassle
  • Property prices are inflated (bubble territory)
  • You can invest the difference disciplinedly
  • You're young and priorities may change

When to Buy

  • You plan to stay 10+ years in the same city
  • Stable income with good job security
  • You want forced savings (EMI discipline)
  • Emotional value of owning your home
  • Tax benefits on home loan interest
  • Hedge against rental inflation
