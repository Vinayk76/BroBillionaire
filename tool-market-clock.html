üìà Understanding Market Trading Hours

Global financial markets operate across different time zones, creating a 24-hour trading environment for forex. Stock markets have specific opening and closing times based on their local time zones.

Forex Market Sessions

The forex market operates 24 hours a day, 5 days a week, through four major trading sessions:

  • Sydney Session (5 PM - 2 AM EST): Opens the trading week, lowest volume session
  • Tokyo Session (7 PM - 4 AM EST): Major Asian session, JPY pairs most active
  • London Session (3 AM - 12 PM EST): Largest session by volume, 35% of daily forex volume
  • New York Session (8 AM - 5 PM EST): High volatility, USD pairs dominate

Best Time to Trade Forex

The London-New York overlap (8 AM - 12 PM EST) is the most active trading period with highest liquidity and volatility. Over 70% of all forex trades happen during this window.

Major Stock Exchange Hours

  • NYSE & NASDAQ: 9:30 AM - 4:00 PM EST (Pre-market: 4 AM, After-hours: 8 PM)
  • London Stock Exchange: 8:00 AM - 4:30 PM GMT
  • NSE & BSE India: 9:15 AM - 3:30 PM IST
  • Tokyo Stock Exchange: 9:00 AM - 3:00 PM JST (Lunch: 11:30 AM - 12:30 PM)
  • Hong Kong Stock Exchange: 9:30 AM - 4:00 PM HKT

Why Market Timing Matters

  • Volatility: Higher during session overlaps - better for scalpers
  • Spreads: Tighter during active hours - lower trading costs
  • Liquidity: Higher during major sessions - easier order execution
  • News Impact: Economic releases align with market hours
