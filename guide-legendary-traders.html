7 Lessons from Legendary Traders

Common principles shared by all these market wizards

1

Risk Management First

Every legend prioritizes not losing money over making money. They cut losses quickly and protect capital at all costs.

2

Bet Big When Right

When conviction is high and the setup is right, they size up aggressively. Small bets can't make you rich.

3

Patience is Everything

They wait for perfect setups and don't force trades. Most of their returns come from a few big winners.

4

Learn from Mistakes

All of them have had spectacular losses. The difference is they learned and improved from each one.

5

Think Independently

They form their own views and have the courage to go against consensus when their analysis differs.

6

Continuous Adaptation

Markets change. What worked 10 years ago may not work today. They constantly evolve their approaches.
