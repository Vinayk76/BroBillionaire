Beta Interpretation:
  • β = 1: Stock moves exactly with the market
  • β > 1: Stock is more volatile than the market (higher risk, higher potential return)
  • β < 1: Stock is less volatile than the market (lower risk, defensive)
  • β = 0: Stock movement is uncorrelated with the market
  • β < 0: Stock moves opposite to the market (rare, inverse relationship)
