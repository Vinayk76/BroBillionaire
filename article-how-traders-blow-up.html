The Resurrection Protocol

Blew up your account? You're not alone. 90% of traders do. Here's how the 10% come back.

1

Autopsy First

Don't deposit more money until you understand exactly what killed you. Review every trade. Find the pattern. It's always there.

2

Paper Trade

Rebuild confidence without risk. If you can't make money with fake money, real money won't help. 3 profitable months minimum.

3

Start Tiny

When you return, trade 1/10th your normal size. Prove you've changed. Scale up only after 50 profitable trades.

4

Rules Are Religion

Write your trading rules. Print them. Read them before every session. One violation = walk away for the day. No exceptions.
