How to calculate in-hand salary from CTC? ‚ñº
In-hand salary = CTC - Employer PF - Gratuity - Employee PF - Professional Tax - Income Tax. Our calculator automatically computes all these deductions based on your CTC and shows exact monthly take-home pay.
What percentage of CTC is the in-hand salary? ‚ñº
Typically, in-hand salary ranges from 60-75% of CTC. For lower CTCs (under 10L), it can be as high as 80%. For higher CTCs (above 20L), it might be 55-65% due to higher tax brackets. The exact percentage depends on your tax regime, investments, and company salary structure.
Which is better - Old Regime or New Regime? ‚ñº
New Regime is better if you don't have significant deductions (less than ‚Çπ3-4 Lakhs). Old Regime is better if you have home loan interest, high HRA claims, and 80C/80D investments totaling more than ‚Çπ4 Lakhs. Use our comparison feature to see which saves you more tax.
How is PF calculated from salary? ‚ñº
PF is calculated as 12% of Basic Salary. Both employee and employer contribute 12% each. Of employer's 12%, 8.33% goes to EPS and 3.67% to EPF. PF is calculated on Basic up to ‚Çπ15,000 for statutory compliance, but many companies calculate on full basic.
