## Content SEO Tips

### For New Articles
1. Include primary keyword in title
2. Use H1 for main title (once)
3. Use H2/H3 for subheadings
4. Include keywords naturally in first 100 words
5. Add internal links to related articles
6. Aim for 1500+ words for comprehensive topics
7. Include FAQ section for featured snippets

### Keyword Strategy
- **Primary**: Trading education, options trading, futures trading
- **Secondary**: Bank Nifty, Nifty options, trading psychology
- **Long-tail**: "How to become profitable trader", "best trading books"
