Should I rebalance my portfolio?

Annual rebalancing forces "sell high, buy low" discipline. If Nvidia surges to 50% of your portfolio, rebalancing sells some and buys laggards. This reduces risk and can boost long-term returns.

Can I add non-Bro Billionaire stocks?

Yes. This simulator focuses on the 7 core names, but you can mentally add index funds or other stocks. Example: 70% Bro Billionaire portfolio + 30% S&P 500 ETF.
