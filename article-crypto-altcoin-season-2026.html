What is Altcoin Season?

Altcoin season (or "altseason") is a period when altcoins dramatically outperform Bitcoin. During these phases, altcoins can rise 10x, 50x, even 100x or more in weeks to months.

Why Does Altseason Happen?

  • Profit Rotation: Bitcoin holders take profits and rotate into higher-risk, higher-reward alts
  • FOMO Cascade: Retail investors see alts pumping and chase gains
  • Narrative Cycles: New narratives (DeFi, NFTs, AI) attract fresh capital
  • Market Euphoria: Risk appetite increases as confidence builds

ETH/BTC Ratio

ETH is the "gateway drug" to altcoins. When ETH outperforms BTC, altseason is likely:

  • Rising ETH/BTC ‚Üí Risk-on, altcoins likely to pump
  • Falling ETH/BTC ‚Üí Risk-off, capital fleeing to BTC safety
  • ETH/BTC at cycle lows ‚Üí Often best time to accumulate alts

Altseason Mistakes That Destroy Portfolios

  • Chasing After the Pump: Buying after 10x is how you become exit liquidity.
  • No Profit-Taking Plan: "I'll sell at $X" never works without discipline.
  • Over-Concentration: Going 100% into one coin = gambling, not investing.
  • Ignoring BTC Dominance: The single most important indicator, often ignored.
  • Leverage in Euphoria: Using leverage when "it can't go down" = liquidation.
  • Not Converting to Stables: Rotating profits to stables locks gains. Paper gains evaporate.
  • Believing "This Time Is Different": Every cycle feels different. Every cycle ends similarly.
