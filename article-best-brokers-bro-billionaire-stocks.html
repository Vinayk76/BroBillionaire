Tax Nightmare: What They Don't Tell You

Double Taxation Reality

  • US TDS (Tax Deducted at Source): 15% on dividends, 25% on capital gains (can be reduced with DTAA treaty)
  • Indian Capital Gains Tax: 20% with indexation (LTCG after 24 months) OR as per your income tax slab (STCG)
  • Foreign Tax Credit: You can claim credit for US tax paid when filing Indian ITR (but paperwork is hell)
  • LRS Reporting: Every transaction must be reported to RBI if total exceeds $250k/year

Good News: Modern brokers auto-handle most of this. Vested and Ind Money file W-8BEN forms automatically, reducing US withholding from 30% to 15%.

Bad News: Indian ITR filing is still your responsibility. Budget ‚Çπ3,000-5,000 for a CA if you have US stocks.
