What is a QR code? ‚ñº
A QR (Quick Response) code is a two-dimensional barcode that can store various types of information like URLs, text, phone numbers, WiFi credentials, and more. It can be scanned instantly using smartphone cameras.

Yes! Our QR code generator is completely free with no limits. You can create unlimited QR codes and download them as PNG images. QR codes themselves are free to use commercially and personally.
Do QR codes expire? ‚ñº
Static QR codes (like the ones we generate) never expire. They're simply encoded data that points to a destination. As long as the destination (URL, email, etc.) is valid, the QR code will work forever.
