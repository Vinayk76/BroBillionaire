The 10 Bear Market Survival Rules

1

Accept That You Didn't Sell the Top

Nobody sells the exact top. Forgive yourself. Focus on what you can control now, not what you "should have done."

2

Reduce Altcoin Exposure Immediately

95%+ of altcoins never recover from bear market lows. Rotate to BTC/ETH or stables. Quality over quantity.

3

Hold Cash ‚Äî It's a Position

Being 100% invested in a bear market is insane. Keep 30-50% in stables for opportunities and mental peace.

4

Delete Trading Apps (Temporarily)

Checking prices 50 times a day only creates anxiety. Set alerts for significant levels and step away.

5

No Leverage. Ever.

Bear markets are designed to liquidate leveraged positions. Random 20% pumps will wipe your shorts. 30% dumps will wipe your longs.

6

Start DCA When Fear Is Maximum

Fear & Greed Index below 20 = accumulation zone. Buy small amounts consistently. Don't try to catch the exact bottom.

7

Focus on Income, Not Gains

Build your income outside crypto. The more fiat you earn, the more you can deploy at lows. Bear market = accumulation period.

8

Learn and Build Skills

Bear markets are for learning: technical analysis, DeFi, coding, content creation. Skills compound for the next cycle.

9

Remember: This Has Happened Before

Every previous bear market was followed by new all-time highs. Bitcoin has gone through 4 bear markets and recovered every single time.

10

Protect Your Mental Health

Money lost can be made back. Your mental health cannot be traded for. Take breaks, exercise, talk to friends. It's just money.

Bear Market DCA Strategy

Dollar-Cost Averaging (DCA) is the best strategy for bear markets. You can't time the bottom, but you can average into it.

Example: $500/month DCA into BTC

Month 1: BTC at $30,000 0.0167 BTC
Month 2: BTC at $25,000 0.0200 BTC
Month 3: BTC at $20,000 0.0250 BTC
Month 4: BTC at $17,000 0.0294 BTC
Total: $2,000 invested 0.0911 BTC @ $21,953 avg

Smart DCA Tips

  • Automate it: Set up recurring buys on Coinbase/Binance so you don't "forget"
  • Heavy when fearful: Double your DCA when Fear & Greed below 20
  • Reserve for capitulation: Keep 30% of DCA budget for "blood in streets" moments
  • Stick to quality: 70% BTC, 20% ETH, 10% conviction alts
  • Don't try to time: Consistent > perfect timing

The Psychology of Bear Markets

Bear markets follow predictable emotional stages. Knowing where you are helps you make better decisions:

Denial (Early Bear)

"This is just a dip. We'll bounce back. I'm not selling."

Anger (Mid Bear)

"Why didn't I sell? These markets are manipulated!"

Bargaining

"If BTC gets back to my entry, I'll sell everything."

Depression (Capitulation)

"Crypto is dead. I should never have invested. Selling everything."

Acceptance (Bottom Forming)

"I lost, but I learned. Let me rebuild slowly."

Disbelief (Early Bull)

"This is just a dead cat bounce. I'm not falling for it again."

The Capitulation Signal

When YOU feel like selling everything and giving up on crypto, that's often the bottom. Peak despair = peak opportunity. If you can hold and buy when it feels the worst, you'll outperform 90% of the market.
